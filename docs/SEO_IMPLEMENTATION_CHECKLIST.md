## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ Keyword stuffing - Keep it natural
2. ❌ Duplicate content - Make each page unique
3. ❌ Slow page speed - Optimize regularly
4. ❌ Ignoring mobile users - Mobile-first approach
5. ❌ Not updating content - Keep data fresh
6. ❌ Poor internal linking - Connect related pages
7. ❌ Ignoring user intent - Focus on what users want
